K12Listing to the rescue

K12Listing provides you the opportunity to sell your old books, games and instruments within your own K12 community who you can trust and have known all along. Just trade or exchange with other students in your own K12 community without going through the re-seller websites. Re-seller websites like Amazon, AbeBooks, Gamestop etc pay you pennies on a dollar and require your time and effort to mail your items. They sell you back these books and games at a much higher price. Now you can post your old text books, games, instruments and do a Fair exchange OR Earn Top dollars and use them to purchase new text books, games or instruments from parents of other students in your K12 community.

How it works

It's simple and easy. You sign up and start posting your items right away. Its free.

The buyer's then contact you via email.

You workout a time and venue to exchange the items with the buyer.

Its a Win Win for both buyers and sellers
