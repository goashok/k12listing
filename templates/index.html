K12 Exchange Program

An idea inspired by a student of Marlboro Memorial Middle School, Marlboro NJ.

Are your kids graduating from their current grade and need new text books?.

You are not sure what to do with their old text books?

Are your kids bored of playing their old game over and over again?

Upgraded your kids musical instrument and have an old to spare?

K12Listing to the rescue

K12 Exchange provides you the opportunity to sell your old books, games and instruments within your own K12 community who you can trust and have known all along without a middle man. Book websites like Amazon, AbeBooks etc pay you pennies on a dollar and require your time and effort to mail your items. You can post your old text books, games, instruments and do a Fair exchange OR Earn top dollars and use them to purchase new text books, games or instruments from parents of other students in your community.
