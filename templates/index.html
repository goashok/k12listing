K12 Exchange Program

A Parent-to-Parent exhange within a community

An idea inspired by a student of Marlboro Memorial Middle School, Marlboro NJ

Kids graduating from their current grade and need new text books?

Not sure what to do with their old text books ?

Kids bored of playing their old game over and over again ?

Upgraded your kids musical instrument and have an old to spare ?

K12 Exchange provides you the opportunity to sell your old books, games and instruments within your own K12 community who you can trust and have known all along without a middle man. Book websites like Amazon, AbeBooks etc pay you pennies on a dollar and require your time and effort to mail your items.

You can post your old text books, games, instruments and do a Fair exchange OR Earn top dollars and use them to purchase new text books, games or instruments from parents of other students in your community.
